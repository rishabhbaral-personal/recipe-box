RECIPE BOX

Arisha Pitha

Oriya Sweet

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c jaggery, 1/4c water, 1tbsp sesame, 1c atta, cardamom.

Method: Soak/grind rice. Boil jaggery to "solid lump" consistency. Mix in rice and atta. Shape, roll in sesame, and fry.

Pudding

Traditional Custard

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1.5tsp oil.

Method: Make caramel in tiffin box. Blend other ingredients. Place tiffin in pressure cooker water bath. 1 whistle then 20m slow flame.

Poda Pitha

Burnt Cake

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c Biri, 1c coconut, jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee, salt, baking powder, cardamom.

Method: Grind soaked rice/biri. Ferment 4hrs. Mix ingredients. Bake in oiled pressure cooker (no whistle) for 1hr on simmer.

Khira Gaintha

Milk Dumplings

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, 2c water, milk (inc evaporated), sugar, cardamom, coconut, nuts.

Method: Boil water with oil/salt, stir in flour to make dough. Form balls. Boil milk until thick, add balls and simmer.

Mango Pie

No-Bake Dessert

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz Philly cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 jello pkts, crust.

Method: Mix cheese, sugar, pulp, and whip. Dissolve jello in hot water, stir into mix. Pour into crust and set overnight.

Watergate Salad

Pistachio Delight

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: Pudding mix, 20oz pineapple, 1c marshmallows, 1/2c nuts, 1.75c whipped topping.

Method: Combine all but topping. Fold in topping. Chill 1 hour.

Besara

Mustard Veg Curry

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, sweet potato, potol, cowpeas, chickpeas, mustard/cumin paste.

Method: Grind mustard/cumin/ginger. Cook veggies in paste and coconut. Temper with ghee and chillies.

Laal Mutton

Red Meat Curry

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, whole chillies, 1.5c ghee, curd, garlic paste, cardamom, cinnamon.

Method: Slow cook mutton in ghee with chilli paste and curd until tender and deep red.

Malai Chicken

Creamy Chicken

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: 1kg Chicken, cashew/poppy seed paste, curd, cream, white pepper, ginger-garlic.

Method: Marinate chicken in curd/cream. Cook with nut paste until rich and velvety.

Birria (Goat)

Mexican Stew

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, roasted tomatoes, vinegar.

Method: Saut√© aromatics. Blend with tomatoes/broth. Pressure cook meat in sauce 45m. Shred and serve.

Kabab

Chicken/Turkey

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground meat, ginger, garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, egg, butter.

Method: Mix/freeze 30m. Shape balls, boil 10m. Dry and flash-fry in butter.

Poha Roll

Rice Flake Snack

Poha Roll

Ingredients: Egg, 30g Poha, 100g potato, chillies, 200g peanuts, onion, amchur.

Method: Mash potato and poha with spices. Form rolls and fry until crisp.

Rashmalai

Quick Dessert

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, Gits Kulfi mix.

Method: Boil milk with kulfi mix. Squeeze rasagollas, drop in hot milk. Serve cold.
