Arisha Pitha

Oriya Tradition

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c Jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame seed, 1c Atta, Cardamom pwd.

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it in the morning, and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Heat water and jaggery until it forms a solid lump that sticks to the bottom when tested in a bowl of water.
  3. Switch off heat and slowly stir rice powder into the syrup constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Add Atta and cardamom powder; mix well to create the "paga".
  5. Within an hour, roll into small circles, coat in sesame seeds, and fry over medium heat.

Pudding

Caramel Custard

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c Milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, 1tsp vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1/2tsp oil.

  1. Heat oil and sugar in a steel tiffin box until light to dark brown to make caramel; stop heat immediately.
  2. Grind eggs, sugar, vanilla, and milk into a mixture.
  3. Add mixture to the tiffin box and close the lid.
  4. Place in a pressure cooker with 1/4 water.
  5. Cook for 1 whistle, then reduce to slow flame for 10-20 mins.
  6. Serve cold by flipping onto a plate.

Watergate Salad

Pistachio Delight

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: 4oz pistachio pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple with juice, 1c marshmallows, 1/2c chopped nuts, 1 3/4c whipped topping.

  1. Stir pudding mix, pineapple, marshmallows, and nuts in a large bowl until blended.

Poda Pitha

Slow-Baked Cake

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram, 1c grated coconut, 3c sugar/jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee, cardamom.

  1. Soak rice and gram for 4 hours; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment both batters for 4 hours, then mix with coconut, ginger, salt, sugar, and cashew.
  3. Pre-heat pressure cooker and grease the inner surface with oil.
  4. Pour batter in and cook without whistle on simmer for one hour.

Khira Gaintha

Rice Flour Dumplings

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, sugar, 7c milk (inc. evaporated), cardamom, coconut, nuts.

  1. Boil water with oil and salt; gradually stir in rice flour to avoid lumps.
  2. Cook covered for 5 mins, cool, knead into a ball, and form small balls to steam.
  3. In a separate pan, boil and thicken milk and evaporated milk.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom, then drop balls in and stir.
  5. Add shredded coconut and nuts; serve cool.

Rashmalai

Quick Version

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, 1 packet Gits Kulfi mix.

  1. Heat milk and evaporated milk for 5 mins; stir in Kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze syrup out of the Rasagollas and drop them into the hot milk.
  3. Ensure they are submerged and warm the whole thing for 2 mins.
  4. Serve cold.

Rasagolla

Soft Chenna Balls

Rasagolla

Ingredients: 3L full cream milk, 3tbsp vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar, cardamom.

  1. Curdle boiling milk with diluted vinegar; strain through thin cloth and hang for one hour.
  2. Knead chenna with flour for 8 mins until smooth and dough-like.
  3. Make syrup with sugar and water; boil balls in syrup for 12 mins (high flame, closed lid) and 5 mins (low flame, open lid).

Mango Pie

No-Bake Creamy Pie

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz Philadelphia cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 Jello packets, Graham cracker crust.

  1. Mix room-temperature cheese with sugar.
  2. Stir in mango pulp and then fold in Cool Whip.
  3. Microwave Jello with 1/2c water for 20s and stir into the mixture.
  4. Pour into the crust and chill for one day before serving.

Cuban Cream Cake

Rishi's Specialty

Cuban Cream Cake

Ingredients: 2c Flour, 2tsp Baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c Heavy Whipping Cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F; grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Sift dry ingredients together.
  3. Whip heavy cream until thickened.
  4. Beat eggs until frothy and fold into the whipped cream.
  5. Gently fold in dry ingredients and vanilla.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out dry.

Laau Ghanta

Bottle Gourd Stew

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients: 12" Laau, 4 potatoes, tomato, onion, 4tbsp coconut, 4tbsp bengal beans, ghee, phutan, ginger, hingu.

  1. Cut Laau and potato into similar shapes.
  2. Heat ghee with bay leaf, phutan, and cardamom; sauté onion and ginger until transparent.
  3. Add potato, laau, coconut, and bengal beans; cover and cook until soft.
  4. Add coriander, tomato, and hingu; cook until reaching desired consistency.

Besara

Mustard Veggie Curry

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, sweet potato, potol, cowpeas, chickpeas, mustard-cumin paste, coconut, jaggery.

  1. Grind mustard, cumin, ginger, and black pepper into a fine paste.
  2. Mix vegetables with the paste, coconut, chickpeas, turmeric, and salt in a pan.
  3. Add water and cook until vegetables are done. Stir in jaggery.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and red chillies; garnish with coriander.

Potala Kurma

Gourd & Chena Curry

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: 5-6 Pointed gourd, onion, ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, coconut milk, chena (from boiled milk), spices.

  1. Prepare chena by curdling boiled milk with lemon; strain and rest.
  2. Scrape gourds, make deep cuts, fry them, and set aside.
  3. Make a paste of green chili and ginger; sauté in oil/ghee with panch phuran.
  4. Add spices, water, yogurt/tomato paste, and stir until water evaporates.
  5. Add water, chena, and fried gourds; cook to desired consistency.

Kabab

Chicken/Turkey

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground meat, ginger, garlic, coriander, curd, Chaplee masala, egg, butter, chaat masala.

  1. Mix ingredients and freeze for 30 mins.
  2. Form into small or oval balls and add to boiling water for 10 mins.

Malai Chicken

Creamy Marination

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, heavy cream, cheese, coriander-mint-chilli paste, kasoori methi, ginger-garlic, lemon, tandoori masala, mustard oil.

  1. 1st Marination: Mix chicken with lemon, salt, and ginger-garlic; rest 20 mins.
  2. 2nd Marination: Mix cream, cheese, herb paste, and spices; add chicken and keep overnight.
  3. Bring to room temperature and broil in oven, flipping occasionally until done.

Birria Goat

Mexican Pulled Meat

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat meat, chili powder, cumin, ginger, cinnamon, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic.

  1. Brown goat chunks in oil and set aside. Sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Toast spices, add broth and charred tomatoes, then blend into a smooth sauce.
  3. Pressure cook meat in sauce on HIGH for 45 mins.
  4. Shred meat with forks and serve in the consommé.
  5. Tortillas: Mix flour, water, and oil; knead, roll into circles, and cook 1 min per side.
