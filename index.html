Arisha Pitha

Return to Contents

Ingredients

3c rice, 2c jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame seeds, 1c Atta, Cardamom powder.

Preparation

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it in the morning, and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Boil water and jaggery until the syrup is thick enough that a drop forms a solid lump in cold water.
  3. Switch off the heat and slowly stir the rice powder into the syrup to avoid any lumps.
  4. Mix in the Atta and cardamom powder to create the dough (paga).
  5. Form small circles, roll them in sesame seeds, and fry over medium heat.

Besara

Return to Contents

Ingredients

Pumpkin, yam, banana, mustard, cumin, ginger, coconut, jaggery.

Preparation

  1. Grind the mustard, cumin, and ginger into a fine paste.
  2. Cook all chopped vegetables with the paste, coconut, and water.
  3. Stir in jaggery for a hint of sweetness once vegetables are tender.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and dried red chillies.

Birria (Goat)

Return to Contents

Ingredients

4lb goat meat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic.

Preparation

  1. Brown the meat chunks in a pan and sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Blend charred tomatoes, broth, and spices into a smooth sauce.
  3. Pressure cook the meat in the sauce on HIGH for 45 minutes.
  4. Shred the meat and serve in the consommé with fresh tortillas.

Coconut Fried Rice

Return to Contents

Ingredients

1c Rice, 1.5c coconut milk, 0.5c water, shahi jeera, salt.

Preparation

  1. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, and jeera in a pot.
  2. Soak the mixture for 45 minutes before cooking.
  3. Cook on low heat until the rice is tender and milk is fully absorbed.

Cuban Cream Cake

Return to Contents

Ingredients

2c Flour, baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

Preparation

  1. Whip the heavy cream until it thickens into soft peaks.
  2. Beat eggs until frothy and fold them gently into the cream.
  3. Gently fold in the dry ingredients and vanilla.
  4. Bake in greased pans at 350°F for 30-40 minutes.

Kabab

Return to Contents

Ingredients

Ground chicken/turkey, ginger-garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, butter, chaat masala.

Preparation

  1. Mix ingredients and freeze for 30 minutes to set the meat.
  2. Form into small balls and boil in water for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and flash-fry in butter for 60 seconds.
  4. Sprinkle with chaat masala before serving hot.

Khira Gaintha

Return to Contents

Ingredients

2c rice flour, sugar, 7c milk (including evaporated), cardamom, coconut, nuts.

Preparation

  1. Stir flour into boiling water with oil and salt to make a dough.
  2. Form dough into small balls and steam them.
  3. Boil milk and evaporated milk until reduced and thick.
  4. Simmer balls in milk; garnish with nuts and shredded coconut.

Laal Mutton

Return to Contents

Ingredients

1kg mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, whole spices, cardamom, onion.

Preparation

  1. Slow cook the mutton in ghee with whole spices and sliced onion.
  2. Add the red chilli paste and curd to develop the deep red gravy.
  3. Simmer on low until the meat is fork-tender.

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients

12" Laau, potato, onion, coconut, black beans, ghee, phutan, hingu.

Preparation

  1. Sauté onion and ginger in ghee with phutan and cardamom.
  2. Add the gourd, potato, coconut, and beans; cook covered until soft.

  4. Reduce the gravy on high heat to reach desired consistency.

Malai Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken, heavy cream, cheese, herb paste, tandoori masala, mustard oil.

Preparation

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon and ginger-garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Coat in cream, cheese, and herb paste; marinate overnight.
  3. Broil in the oven, flipping once, until fully cooked.

Mango Pie

Return to Contents

Ingredients

15oz pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 jello pkts, crust.

Preparation

  1. Mix softened cheese and sugar; add mango pulp and fold in Cool Whip.
  2. Microwave jello in 1/2c water for 20s and stir into the mixture.
  3. Pour into the crust and chill for 24 hours.

Poda Pitha

Return to Contents

Ingredients

4c rice, 2c black gram, 1c coconut, 3c sugar/jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee.

Preparation

  1. Soak rice and gram for 4 hours; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment for 4 hours, then mix with coconut, ginger, and sugar.
  3. Pour into oiled pressure cooker and cook on simmer for 1 hour without the whistle.

Potala Kurma

Return to Contents

Ingredients

Gourd, chena, onion, ginger-garlic, yogurt, coconut milk, ghee.

Preparation

  1. Fry slitted gourds; prepare fresh chena from boiled milk and lemon.
  2. Sauté spices and ginger-chili paste; add yogurt and cook until dry.
  3. Mix in chena, gourds, and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer to thicken and garnish with a touch of cream.

Potol Rasha

Return to Contents

Ingredients

500g potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

Preparation

  1. Fry the gourd rounds in ghee until golden; set aside.
  2. Sauté the masala paste in ghee with phutan and bay leaf.
  3. Simmer the gourds in coconut milk until the oil separates.

Pudding

Return to Contents

Ingredients

2c milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, 1tsp vanilla, caramel sugar.

Preparation

  1. Melt caramel sugar and oil in a steel tiffin box until light brown.
  2. Grind eggs, sugar, vanilla, and milk into a smooth mixture.
  3. Add mixture to the tiffin box and close the lid tightly.
  4. Steam in a pressure cooker water bath for one whistle, then 15 minutes slow flame.

Rajma

Return to Contents

Ingredients

2c boiled beans, 1tbsp besan, ghee, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

Preparation

  1. Heat ghee and temper with bay leaf and jeera.
  2. Add besan and fry until it turns reddish and fragrant.
  3. Add the aromatics, tomatoes, and boiled beans; simmer until thick.

Rasagolla

Return to Contents

Ingredients

3L full cream milk, vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar.

Preparation

  1. Curdle boiling milk with vinegar; strain through cloth and hang for 1 hour.
  2. Knead the chenna with flour for 8 minutes until it is smooth dough.
  3. Boil balls in sugar syrup for 12 mins on high and 5 mins on low.

Rashmalai

Return to Contents

Ingredients

1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, kulfi mix.

Preparation

  1. Boil the milk and evaporated milk together; stir in the kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze the syrup out of the Rasagollas and drop them into the milk.
  3. Simmer for 2 minutes and serve chilled.

Suji Cake

Return to Contents

Ingredients

3c suji, 3c buttermilk, 3/4c oil, 3/4c sugar, 1tsp soda, 1c coconut.

Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Let the mixture sit at room temperature for 2 hours.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for exactly one hour.

Watergate Salad

Return to Contents

Ingredients

4oz pistachio pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple, 1c marshmallows, 1/2c chopped nuts, 1.75c whipped topping.

Preparation

  1. Stir pudding mix, pineapple with juice, marshmallows, and nuts in a large bowl.
  2. Gently fold in the whipped topping until smooth.
  3. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
