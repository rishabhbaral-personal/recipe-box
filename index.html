Handwritten Recipes

Arisha Pitha

Traditional Oriya sweet circles fried with sesame seeds and jaggery.

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame seeds, 1c Atta, Cardamom powder.

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it in the morning, and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Boil water and jaggery until the syrup is thick enough that a drop forms a solid lump in cold water.
  3. Switch off the heat and slowly stir the rice powder into the syrup to avoid any lumps.
  4. Mix in the Atta and cardamom powder to create the dough (paga).
  5. Form small circles, roll them in sesame seeds, and fry over medium heat.

Pudding

A classic steamed caramel custard that melts in your mouth.

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, 1tsp vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1/2tsp oil.

  1. Heat oil and sugar in a steel tiffin box until it turns a light brown caramel; stop heat immediately.
  2. Grind eggs, sugar, vanilla, and milk in a blender until well mixed.
  3. Pour the mixture into the tiffin box and close the lid tightly.
  4. Place the tiffin in a pressure cooker with 1/4 water bath.
  5. Cook for 1 whistle, then reduce to a slow flame for 10-20 minutes.

Watergate Salad

The "Pistachio Delight" - a classic mid-century fluffy dessert salad.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: 4oz pistachio pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple, 1c marshmallows, 1/2c chopped nuts, 1.75c whipped topping.

  1. Stir the dry pudding mix, pineapple (with juice), marshmallows, and nuts in a bowl.
  2. Gently fold in the whipped topping until smooth.
  3. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.

Poda Pitha

A slow-baked rice and gram cake traditional to the Raja festival.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram, 1c coconut, 3c sugar/jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee, cardamom.

  1. Soak rice and gram for 4 hours; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment the batter for 4 hours, then mix with coconut, ginger, sugar, and cashews.
  3. Grease a pressure cooker with oil or ghee.
  4. Pour in the batter and cook on simmer for 1 hour without the whistle.

Khira Gaintha

Rice flour dumplings simmered in thick, cardamom-scented milk.

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, sugar, 7c milk (including evaporated), cardamom, coconut, nuts.

  1. Boil water with salt/oil and stir in rice flour to make a smooth dough.
  2. Form the dough into small balls and steam them.
  3. Thicken the milk and evaporated milk together in a pan.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom, then simmer the balls in the milk.
  5. Top with shredded coconut and nuts.

Rashmalai

Spongy Rasagollas reimagined in a creamy Kulfi sauce.

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, Gits Kulfi mix.

  1. Boil the milk and evaporated milk together; stir in the Kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze the syrup out of the Rasagollas and drop them into the milk.
  3. Simmer for 2 minutes to let them absorb the flavors.
  4. Serve chilled.

Rasagolla

Soft, spongy white balls of fresh chenna in light sugar syrup.

Rasagolla

Ingredients: 3L full cream milk, 3tbsp vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar, cardamom.

  1. Curdle boiling milk with vinegar; strain through cloth and hang for 1 hour.
  2. Knead the chenna with flour for 8 minutes until it is smooth dough.
  3. Boil sugar and water to make syrup; boil balls for 12 mins on high and 5 mins on low.

Mango Pie

A luscious, no-bake cream cheese pie featuring sweet mango pulp.

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 jello pkts, crust.

  1. Mix softened cheese and sugar; add mango pulp and fold in Cool Whip.
  2. Microwave jello in 1/2c water for 20s and stir into the mixture.
  3. Pour into the Graham crust and chill for 24 hours.

Fruit Pudding

The ultimate instant dessert using mango and condensed milk.

Quick Fruit Pudding

Ingredients: 1 tin mango pulp, 1 tin condensed milk, 1 container whip cream, fresh fruits.

  1. Whisk the mango pulp and condensed milk until combined.
  2. Add the whip cream and mix well.
  3. Garnish with fresh fruits and serve chilled or frozen.

Suji Cake

A dense, coconut-flavored semolina cake with buttermilk.

Suji Cake

Ingredients: 3c suji, 3c buttermilk, 3/4c oil, 3/4c sugar, 1tsp soda, 1c coconut, cardamom.

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Let the mixture sit at room temperature for 2 hours.
  3. Bake at 350°F for exactly one hour.

Cuban Cream Cake

An incredibly rich sponge cake using heavy cream instead of butter.

Cuban Cream Cake

Ingredients: 2c Flour, baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

  1. Whip the heavy cream until it thickens.
  2. Beat eggs until frothy and fold them into the cream.
  3. Gently fold in the dry ingredients and vanilla.
  4. Bake in greased pans at 350°F for 30-40 minutes.

Rajma

Comforting red kidney beans thickened with a hint of roasted besan.

Rajma

Ingredients: 2c boiled beans, 1tbsp besan, ghee, green chilli, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

  1. Heat ghee and temper with bay leaf and jeera.
  2. Add besan and fry until it turns reddish and fragrant.
  3. Add the aromatics, tomatoes, and boiled beans; simmer to thicken.

Laau Ghanta

Mixed vegetable stew featuring bottle gourd, beans, and coconut.

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients: 12" Laau, potato, onion, coconut, bengal black beans, ghee, phutan, hingu.

  1. Sauté onion and ginger in ghee with phutan and cardamom.
  2. Add the gourd, potato, coconut, and beans; cook covered until soft.
  3. Stir in tomato, hingu, and coriander.
  4. Reduce the gravy on high heat to reach desired consistency.

Besara

A classic mustard-paste curry loaded with tropical vegetables.

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, mustard/cumin paste, chickpeas, coconut, jaggery.

  1. Grind the mustard, cumin, and ginger into a fine paste.
  2. Cook all vegetables with the paste, coconut, and water.
  3. Stir in jaggery for a hint of sweetness.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and dried red chillies.

Potala Kurma

Rich pointed gourd curry with homemade chena and coconut milk.

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: Gourd, chena, onion, ginger-garlic, yogurt, coconut milk, ghee, garam masala.

  1. Fry gourds with deep slits; make fresh chena from milk.
  2. Sauté spices and ginger-chili paste; add yogurt and cook until dry.
  3. Mix in chena, gourds, and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer to thicken and garnish with cream.

Laal Mutton

Deep red, fiery meat curry slow-cooked in a rich ghee base.

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, whole spices, cardamom.

  1. Slow cook the mutton in ghee with whole spices and sliced onions.
  2. Add the red chilli paste and curd to create the deep color.
  3. Simmer on low heat until the meat is fork-tender.

Poha Roll

Crispy potato-rice flake rolls packed with roasted peanuts.

Poha Roll

Ingredients: 30g Poha, 100g potato, 200g peanut, onion, amchur, 1 egg.

  1. Mash potato and mix with soaked poha, onion, and spices.
  2. Form into logs or rolls and dip in beaten egg.
  3. Deep fry or shallow fry until golden and crispy.

Kabab

Juicy ground meat kebabs, first boiled and then flash-fried in butter.

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground chicken/turkey, ginger-garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, butter, chaat masala.

  1. Mix ingredients and freeze for 30 minutes to set.
  2. Form balls and boil in water for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and flash-fry in butter for a few seconds.
  4. Sprinkle with chaat masala before serving.

Potol Rasha

Sautéed pointed gourds simmered in a creamy coconut milk gravy.

Potol Rasha

Ingredients: 500g Potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

  1. Fry the gourd rounds in ghee until golden; set aside.
  2. Sauté the masala paste with phutan and bay leaf.
  3. Simmer the gourds in coconut milk until the oil separates.

Malai Chicken

Velvety chicken marinated in cream and cheese, then broiled until golden.

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, cream, cheese, herb paste, tandoori masala, mustard oil.

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix with cream, cheese, and herbs; leave overnight.
  3. Broil in the oven, flipping once, until fully cooked.

Coconut Fried Rice

Fragrant rice soaked in rich coconut milk and spiced with shahi jeera.

Coconut Fried Rice

Ingredients: 1c Rice, 1.5c coconut milk, 0.5c water, shahi jeera, salt.

  1. Soak rice in the coconut milk, water, and jeera for 45 mins.
  2. Cook on low heat until the rice is fluffy and tender.

Birria

Mexican slow-cooked goat meat stew served with fresh handmade tortillas.

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, roasted tomatoes, flour.

  1. Brown the meat; sauté onion and spices.
  2. Blend the sauce with charred tomatoes and broth.
  3. Pressure cook for 45 minutes on high.
  4. Shred meat and serve in the consommé with fresh tortillas.
