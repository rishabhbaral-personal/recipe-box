Arisha Pitha

Arisha Pitha

Traditional Oriya sweet circles fried with sesame seeds and jaggery.

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame, 1c Atta, Cardamom.

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it, and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Boil water and jaggery until the drop forms a solid lump in cold water.
  3. Switch off the heat and stir in rice powder and Atta slowly.
  4. Mix in cardamom powder.
  5. Roll into small circles, coat in sesame, and fry until golden.
Besara

Besara

Mixed vegetables cooked in a pungent mustard-cumin paste.

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, mustard, cumin, ginger, coconut, jaggery.

  1. Grind mustard, cumin, ginger, and black pepper into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook chopped vegetables with the paste, coconut, and water.
  3. Stir in jaggery for balance once the veggies are tender.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and dried red chillies.
Birria

Birria (Goat)

Mexican slow-cooked goat meat stew served with fresh tortillas.

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat meat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic.

  1. Brown the meat chunks and sauté onions/garlic.
  2. Blend sauce with charred tomatoes, broth, and spices.
  3. Pressure cook for 45 minutes on high.
  4. Shred meat and serve in the consommé with fresh tortillas.
Coconut Rice

Coconut Fried Rice

Fragrant rice soaked in rich coconut milk and spiced with shahi jeera.

Coconut Fried Rice

Ingredients: 1c Rice, 1.5c coconut milk, 0.5c water, 1tbsp shahi jeera, salt.

  1. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, and jeera.
  2. Soak the mixture for 45 minutes.
  3. Cook on low heat until the rice is tender and milk is absorbed.
Cream Cake

Cuban Cream Cake

An incredibly rich sponge cake using heavy cream instead of butter.

Cuban Cream Cake

Ingredients: 2c Flour, baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

  1. Whip the heavy cream until it thickens.
  2. Beat eggs until frothy and fold them into the cream.
  3. Gently fold in the dry ingredients and vanilla.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 30-40 minutes.
Kabab

Kabab

Chicken or turkey kebabs, boiled then flash-fried in butter.

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground meat, ginger, garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, butter, chaat masala.

  1. Mix ingredients and freeze for 30 minutes.
  2. Form balls and boil in water for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and flash-fry in butter.
  4. Sprinkle with chaat masala.
Khira Gaintha

Khira Gaintha

Rice flour dumplings simmered in cardamom-scented milk.

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, sugar, 7c milk (inc. evaporated), cardamom, coconut, nuts.

  1. Boil water with salt/oil and stir in flour to make a dough.
  2. Form dough into small balls and steam them.
  3. Boil milk and evaporated milk until reduced and thick.
  4. Simmer balls in milk; garnish with nuts and coconut.
Laal Mutton

Laal Mutton

Deep red, fiery meat curry slow-cooked in a rich ghee base.

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, whole spices, onion.

  1. Slow cook the mutton in ghee with whole spices and onions.
  2. Add the red chilli paste and curd to create the deep color.
  3. Simmer on low heat until the meat is fork-tender.
Laau Ghanta

Laau Ghanta

Mixed vegetable stew featuring bottle gourd, beans, and coconut.

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients: 12" Laau, potato, onion, coconut, black beans, ghee, phutan, hingu.

  1. Sauté onion and ginger in ghee with phutan and cardamom.
  2. Add the gourd, potato, coconut, and beans; cook covered until soft.
  3. Stir in tomato, hingu, and coriander.
  4. Reduce the gravy on high heat to reach desired consistency.
Malai Chicken

Malai Chicken

Velvety chicken marinated in cream and cheese, then broiled until golden.

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, heavy cream, cheese, herb paste, methi, mustard oil.

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Coat in cream, cheese, herb paste, and oil; rest overnight.
  3. Broil in the oven, flipping once, until fully cooked.
Mango Pie

Mango Pie

Refreshing, no-bake mango cream cheese pie.

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz cool whip, jello, crust.

  1. Mix softened cheese and sugar; add pulp and fold in whip.
  2. Dissolve jello in 1/2c hot water and stir into the mix.
  3. Pour into Graham crust and chill for 24 hours.
Poda Pitha

Poda Pitha

Slow-baked rice and gram cake traditional to the Raja festival.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram, 1c coconut, 3c sugar/jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee, cardamom.

  1. Soak rice and gram for 4 hours; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment the batter for 4 hours, then mix with coconut, ginger, and sugar.
  3. Pour into oiled pressure cooker and cook on simmer for 1 hour without the whistle.

Poha Roll

Poha Roll

Potala Kurma

Potala Kurma

Pointed gourd curry with homemade chena and coconut milk.

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: Gourd, chena, onion, ginger-garlic, yogurt, coconut milk, ghee.

  1. Fry gourds with deep slits; make fresh chena from milk.

Potol Rasha

Sautéed pointed gourds simmered in a creamy coconut milk gravy.

Potol Rasha

Ingredients: 500g Potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

Pudding

Pudding

Velvety caramel custard steamed to perfection in a tiffin box.

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, 1tsp vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1/2tsp oil.

  1. Prepare caramel in a tiffin box until dark brown.
  2. Blend eggs, sugar, milk, and vanilla in a grinder.
  3. Steam tiffin in a pressure cooker water bath.
  4. Cook for 1 whistle, then 15 minutes on a slow flame.

Rajma

Comforting red kidney beans thickened with a hint of roasted besan.

Rajma

Ingredients: 2c boiled rajma, 1tbsp besan, ghee, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

Rasagolla

Rasagolla

Soft, spongy white balls of fresh homemade chenna in sugar syrup.

Rasagolla

Ingredients: 3L milk, 3tbsp vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar, cardamom.

  1. Curdle boiling milk with vinegar; strain and hang for 1 hour.
  2. Knead the chenna with flour for 8 minutes until smooth.
  3. Boil balls in sugar syrup for 12 mins on high and 5 mins on low.
Rashmalai

Rashmalai

Spongy Rasagollas reimagined in a rich and creamy Kulfi sauce.

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, Gits Kulfi mix.

  1. Heat milk and evaporated milk; stir in the Kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze the syrup from the Rasagollas and drop them into the milk.
  3. Warm for 2 minutes to merge flavors; serve cold.
Suji Cake

Suji Cake

Dense semolina cake with coconut and cardamom, baked to a golden brown.

Suji Cake

Ingredients: 3c suji, 3c buttermilk, 3/4c oil, 3/4c sugar, 1tsp soda, 1c coconut.

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Let the batter sit at room temperature for 2 hours.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for exactly one hour.
Watergate

Watergate Salad

A classic fluffy pistachio and pineapple dessert salad.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: 4oz pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple, 1c marshmallow, nuts, 1.75c whip.

  1. Stir pudding mix, pineapple with juice, marshmallows, and nuts until blended.
  2. Gently fold in the whipped topping.
  3. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
