Arisha Pitha

Index

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice [cite: 3]
  • 2 cups Jaggery [cite: 4]
  • ¾ cup water [cite: 5]
  • 1 tbsp sesame seed [cite: 6]
  • 1 cup Atta [cite: 7]
  • Cardamom pwd [cite: 8]

Method

  1. Soak rice overnight and dry. Grind to a fine powder[cite: 9].
  2. Heat water and jaggery until the drop forms a solid lump in water[cite: 10].
  3. Switch off heat and stir in rice powder slowly[cite: 11].
  4. Add Atta and cardamom; mix well to make the paga[cite: 12].
  5. Within an hour, roll circles in sesame and fry over medium heat[cite: 13].

Khira Gaintha

Index

Ingredients

2 cups Rice flour, 1 cup milk, 6 cups extra milk, sugar, cardamom powder, coconut, nuts [cite: 63-72].

Method

  1. Boil water with oil and salt[cite: 74, 75].
  2. Gradually add rice flour and stir; cook covered for 5 mins[cite: 76, 77].
  3. Cool mixture and knead into a ball; form small balls and steam [cite: 78-80].
