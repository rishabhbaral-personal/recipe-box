Arisha Pitha

Traditional sweet from Orissa made for festivals.

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame, 1c Atta, Cardamom.

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it, and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Boil water and jaggery until it reaches a consistency where it forms a solid lump in cold water.
  3. Turn off heat and stir in rice powder and Atta slowly.
  4. Mix in cardamom powder to complete the paga.
  5. Form small circles, coat in sesame, and fry until golden.

Besara

A vegetable medley in a pungent mustard gravy.

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, mustard, cumin, ginger, coconut, jaggery.

  1. Grind mustard, cumin, ginger, and black pepper into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook chopped vegetables with the paste, coconut, and water.
  3. Add jaggery for balance once the veggies are tender.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and dried red chillies.

Birria (Goat)

Slow-cooked Mexican stew with aromatic spices.

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic.

  1. Brown the meat chunks and sauté onions/garlic.
  2. Blend sauce with charred tomatoes, broth, and spices.
  3. Pressure cook for 45 minutes on high.
  4. Shred the meat and serve in the rich consommé with handmade tortillas.

Coconut Fried Rice

Fragrant rice cooked in rich coconut milk.

Coconut Fried Rice

Ingredients: 1c Rice, 1.5c coconut milk, 0.5c water, shahi jeera, salt.

  1. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, and jeera.
  2. Soak the mixture for 45 minutes.
  3. Cook on low heat until the rice is tender and milk is absorbed.

Cuban Cream Cake

A decadent sponge cake made with whipped cream.

Cuban Cream Cake

Ingredients: 2c Flour, baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

  1. Whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Beat eggs until frothy and fold into the cream.
  3. Gently fold in sifted dry ingredients and vanilla.
  4. Bake in greased pans at 350°F for 30-40 minutes.

Kabab

Juicy ground meat bites boiled and flash-fried.

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground meat, ginger-garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, butter, chaat masala.

  1. Mix ingredients and freeze for 30 minutes.
  2. Form into balls and boil in water for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and flash-fry in butter for one minute.
  4. Finish with a sprinkle of chaat masala.

Khira Gaintha

Milk-based dumplings flavored with cardamom.

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, sugar, 7c milk (including evaporated), coconut, nuts.

  1. Stir flour into boiling water/oil/salt to make a dough.
  2. Form balls and steam them.
  3. Boil milk and evaporated milk until thickened.
  4. Add balls and simmer; garnish with nuts and coconut.

Laal Mutton

Deep red, spicy goat curry slow-cooked in ghee.

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, cardamom, onions.

  1. Slow cook mutton with ghee, whole spices, and onion.
  2. Add chilli paste and curd for the signature red gravy.
  3. Simmer until meat is tender and gravy is rich.

Laau Ghanta

Traditional bottle gourd stew with black beans.

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients: 12" Laau, potato, onion, coconut, black beans, ghee, phutan, hingu.

  1. Sauté onion and ginger in ghee with phutan and cardamom.
  2. Add gourd, potato, coconut, and beans; cook until soft.
  3. Stir in tomato, hingu, and coriander.
  4. Reduce gravy on high heat to thicken.

Malai Chicken

Creamy marinated chicken with herb paste.

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, cream, cheese, herb paste, tandoori masala, mustard oil.

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon and ginger-garlic for 30 mins.
  2. Coat in cream, cheese, and herb paste; marinate overnight.
  3. Broil in oven, flipping once, until fully cooked.

Mango Pie

Refreshing, no-bake mango cream cheese pie.

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, jello, crust.

  1. Mix softened cheese and sugar; add pulp and fold in Cool Whip.
  2. Dissolve jello in 1/2c hot water and stir into the mix.
  3. Pour into Graham crust and chill for 24 hours.

Poda Pitha

Fermented rice cake baked slow for the Raja festival.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram, 1c coconut, sugar, ginger, cashew, ghee.

  1. Soak rice/gram 4h; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment for 4h and mix with other ingredients.
  3. Pour into oiled pressure cooker.
  4. Cook on simmer for 1 hour without the whistle.

Poha Roll

Crispy potato rolls made with soaked rice flakes.

Poha Roll

Ingredients: 30g Poha, 100g potato, 200g peanut, onion, amchur, egg.

  1. Mash potato and mix with soaked poha and spices.
  2. Form into logs and coat with beaten egg.
  3. Fry until golden and very crispy.

Potala Kurma

Gourd in a rich yogurt and coconut milk base.

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: Gourd, chena, onion, ginger-garlic, yogurt, coconut milk, ghee.

  1. Fry slitted gourds; prepare fresh chena.
  2. Sauté ginger-chili paste and add yogurt until dry.
  3. Add chena, gourds, and coconut milk; simmer.
  4. Finish with a dash of cream.

Potol Rasha

Fried pointed gourds in coconut gravy.

Potol Rasha

Ingredients: 500g Potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

  1. Fry gourd pieces in ghee; set aside.
  2. Sauté masala paste in ghee with phutan and bay leaf.
  3. Simmer gourds in coconut milk until oil separates.

Pudding

Silky caramel custard made in a tiffin box.

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1/2tsp oil.

  1. Prepare caramel in a tiffin box until dark brown.
  2. Blend eggs, sugar, milk, and vanilla.
  3. Steam tiffin in pressure cooker with 1/4 water.
  4. Cook for 1 whistle, then 15 minutes on slow flame.

Quick Fruit Pudding

Fast mango cream with fresh fruit.

Quick Fruit Pudding

Ingredients: 1 tin mango pulp, 1 tin condensed milk, 1 container whip cream.

  1. Whisk mango pulp and condensed milk.
  2. Stir in whip cream.
  3. Garnish with fruit and serve chilled or frozen.

Rajma

Protein-packed beans thickened with besan.

Rajma

Ingredients: 2c beans, 1tbsp besan, ghee, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

  1. Sauté bay leaf and jeera in ghee.
  2. Fry besan until reddish and fragrant.
  3. Add aromatics, tomatoes, and beans; simmer until thick.

Rasagolla

Handmade cheese balls in light sugar syrup.

Rasagolla

Ingredients: 3L milk, 3tbsp vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar.

  1. Curdle milk with vinegar; hang chena for 1 hour.
  2. Knead chena with flour until smooth.
  3. Boil balls in sugar syrup for 12 mins high, 5 mins low.

Rashmalai

Rasagollas served in a creamy Kulfi sauce.

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, kulfi mix.

  1. Boil milk and evaporated milk with kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze syrup from Rasagollas and drop into milk.
  3. Simmer for 2 minutes and serve cold.

Suji Cake

Semolina cake with coconut and buttermilk.

Suji Cake

Ingredients: 3c suji, 3c buttermilk, 3/4c oil, 3/4c sugar, soda, coconut.

  1. Mix all ingredients and rest for 2 hours.
  2. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for one hour.

Watergate Salad

Sweet and fluffy pistachio dessert salad.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: Pistachio pudding mix, pineapple, marshmallows, nuts, whipped topping.

  1. Stir pudding mix, pineapple (with juice), marshmallows, and nuts.
  2. Fold in whipped topping gently.
  3. Chill for at least one hour before serving.
