NADINE'S FAVORITE RECIPES

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c Jaggery, 1/4c water, 1 tbsp sesame seed, 1c Atta, Cardamom pwd.

Method: Soak rice overnight. Boil jaggery in water until it reaches a "solid lump" consistency. Mix in rice powder and atta slowly. Shape into circles, roll in sesame, and fry.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram (Biri), 1c grated coconut, coconut slices, 3c jaggery, 50g ginger, cashew, ghee, salt, baking powder, cardamom/bay leaf.

Method: Grind soaked rice (coarse) and biri (fine). Ferment for 4 hours. Mix all ingredients. Pour into oiled pressure cooker and cook on simmer for 1 hour without whistle.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: 4oz pistachio pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple with juice, 1c marshmallows, 1/2c chopped nuts, 1 3/4c whipped topping.

Method: Stir pudding, pineapple, marshmallows, and nuts. Fold in whipped topping and refrigerate for one hour.

Khira Gaintha (Kheer Puli)

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, 7c total milk (inc. evaporated), 3/4c sugar, cardamom, coconut, cashews, almonds.

Method: Boil water with oil/salt, stir in rice flour to make dough. Form small balls and steam. Thicken milk with sugar/cardamom, add balls, and garnish with nuts.

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz Philadelphia cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 Jello packets, Graham cracker crust.

Method: Mix cheese (room temp) with sugar, pulp, and whipped cream. Dissolve Jello in hot water, mix in, pour into crust, and set overnight.

Birria (Mexican Pulled Goat)

Ingredients: 4lb goat meat, chili powder, cumin, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, onion, 8 garlic cloves, chicken broth, fire-roasted tomatoes, oregano.

Method: Sear meat. Sauté onion/garlic/spices. Add broth and blend sauce until smooth. Pressure cook meat for 45 mins. Shred and serve in consommé.

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, raw banana, sweet potato, potol, cowpeas, chickpeas, brinjal, coconut, mustard/cumin seeds, ginger, black pepper, jaggery.

Method: Grind mustard/cumin/ginger into paste. Cook veggies with paste and coconut. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and red chillies.

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, whole/crushed red chillies, 1.5c ghee, curd, garlic paste, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves, coriander powder.

Method: A rich, spicy mutton curry characterized by its deep red color from dried chillies and slow cooking in ghee.

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: Pointed gourd (Potala), onion, tomato, ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, coconut milk, chena (curd cheese), garam masala.

Method: Fry gourds. Make a paste of ginger/chili. Sauté spices, add yogurt, water, and crumbled chena. Add gourds and simmer until thick.

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground chicken/turkey, ginger, garlic, dhaniya, ajwain, curd, Shan Chaplee masala, egg.

Method: Mix and freeze for 30 mins. Shape into balls and boil for 10 mins. Strain, dry, and fry in butter for a minute. Garnish with chaat masala.

Poha Roll

Ingredients: 1 egg, 30g Poha, 100g potato, green chillies, 200g roasted peanut, onion, amchur powder.

Method: Combine ingredients to form rolls, utilizing the texture of flattened rice (poha) and potatoes.

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, 1 packet Gits Kulfi mix.

Method: Heat milk and kulfi mix. Squeeze syrup from rasagollas and drop into hot milk. Warm for 2 mins and serve cold.
