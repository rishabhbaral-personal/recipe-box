Arisha Pitha

Back to Index

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 2 cups Jaggery
  • ¾ cup water
  • 1 tbsp sesame seed
  • 1 cup Atta (wheat flour)
  • Cardamom powder

Method

  1. Soak rice overnight and dry it in the morning. When almost dry, grind it to a fine powder.
  2. Heat water and add jaggery. Boil until the syrup is thick; when a drop is placed in cold water, it should stick to the bottom and form a solid lump.
  3. Switch off the heat and stir the rice powder into the syrup slowly to ensure no lumps form.
  4. Gradually add Atta and finally the cardamom powder; mix well to create the paga.
  5. Within an hour, form small circles, roll in sesame seeds, and fry over medium heat.

Besara

Back to Index

Ingredients

Pumpkin, yam, raw banana, sweet potato, potol, cowpeas, brinjal, chickpeas, mustard seeds, cumin, ginger, black pepper.

Method

  1. Grind mustard, cumin, ginger, and black pepper into a fine paste.
  2. Mix the cut vegetables with the paste, coconut, chickpeas, turmeric, and salt in a pan.
  3. Add water and cook until the vegetables are tender. Stir in jaggery.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and red chillies. Garnish with coriander.

Birria (Goat)

Back to Index

Ingredients

3-4 lbs goat meat, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic, cumin, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, chili powder.

Method

  1. Season and brown meat in batches; set aside.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic; stir in spices and toast.
  3. Blend charred tomatoes and broth into a smooth sauce.
  4. Pressure cook meat in the sauce for 45 minutes on high.
  5. Shred meat and serve in the consommé with fresh tortillas.

Cuban Cream Cake

Back to Index

Ingredients

2 cups Flour, 2 tsp baking powder, 1½ cups sugar, 1½ cups heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Whip the heavy cream until thickened.
  3. Beat eggs until frothy; fold them into the cream.
  4. Gently fold in sifted dry ingredients and vanilla.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out dry.

Kabab

Back to Index

Ingredients

Ground chicken/turkey, ginger, garlic, curd, kabab masala, egg, butter, chaat masala.

Method

  1. Mix meat and spices; freeze for 30 minutes to set.
  2. Form into small balls and boil in water for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and flash-fry in butter for one minute.
  4. Serve hot sprinkled with chaat masala.

Khira Gaintha

Back to Index

Ingredients

2 cups rice flour, milk, evaporated milk, sugar, cardamom, coconut, nuts.

Method

  1. Boil water with oil and salt; stir in flour to make dough.
  2. Form small balls and steam them.
  3. Thicken milk and evaporated milk; add sugar and cardamom.
  4. Simmer the balls in the milk; garnish with nuts and coconut.

Laal Mutton

Back to Index

Ingredients

1kg mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, whole spices, onion.

Method

  1. Slow cook the mutton in ghee with whole spices and onion.
  2. Add red chilli paste and curd to create the deep red gravy.
  3. Simmer on low until the meat is fork-tender.

Laau Ghanta

Back to Index

Ingredients

12” bottle gourd (Laau), potatoes, tomato, onion, coconut, black beans, ghee, phutan.

Method

  1. Cut gourd and potatoes into similar sizes.
  2. Sauté onion and ginger in ghee with phutan and cardamom.
  3. Add vegetables, coconut, and beans; cook covered until soft.
  4. Add tomato and hingu; cook until reaching desired consistency.

Malai Chicken

Back to Index

Ingredients

Chicken, heavy cream, cheese, herb paste, tandoori masala, mustard oil, lemon.

Method

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon and ginger-garlic for 20 minutes.
  2. Coat in cream, cheese, and herb paste; marinate overnight.
  3. Broil in the oven until fully cooked, flipping once.

Mango Pie

Back to Index

Ingredients

15oz pulp, ½ cup sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz whip, 2 jello pkts, crust.

Method

  1. Mix cheese and sugar; add mango pulp and fold in whip.
  2. Microwave jello in ½ cup water for 20s and stir in.
  3. Pour into Graham crust and chill for 24 hours.

Poda Pitha

Back to Index

Ingredients

4 cups rice, 2 cups black gram, coconut, ginger, cashew, ghee, 3 cups sugar.

Method

  1. Soak rice and gram for 4 hours; grind gram fine and rice coarse.
  2. Ferment for 4 hours; mix with remaining ingredients.
  3. Bake in oiled pressure cooker on simmer for one hour (no whistle).

Potol Rasha

Back to Index

Ingredients

500g potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

Method

  1. Fry gourd rounds in ghee; set aside.
  2. Sauté masala paste with phutan and bay leaf.
  3. Simmer gourds in coconut milk until oil separates.

Pudding

Back to Index

Ingredients

2 cups milk, 4 eggs, 7 tsp sugar, vanilla, caramel sugar.

Method

  1. Prepare caramel in a tiffin box until dark brown.
  2. Grind eggs, sugar, milk, and vanilla into a mixture.
  3. Steam tiffin box in pressure cooker water bath for one whistle, then 15 minutes slow flame.

Rajma

Back to Index

Ingredients

2 cups boiled beans, 1 tbsp besan, ghee, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

Method

  1. Heat ghee and temper with bay leaf and jeera.
  2. Fry besan until reddish; add aromatics and tomatoes.
  3. Add beans and simmer until thickened.

Rasagolla

Back to Index

Ingredients

3L milk, vinegar, 1.5 tbsp flour, 1.5 cups sugar, cardamom.

Method

  1. Curdle milk with vinegar; strain and hang for one hour.
  2. Knead chenna with flour until smooth.
  3. Boil balls in sugar syrup for 12 minutes high and 5 minutes low flame.
