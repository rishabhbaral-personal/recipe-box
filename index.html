Secret Recipes

Arisha Pitha

A classic Oriya delicacy made with jaggery and rice.

Arisha Pitha

Ingredients: 3c rice, 2c Jaggery, 3/4c water, 1tbsp sesame, 1c Atta, Cardamom.

  1. Soak rice overnight, dry it, and grind into fine powder.
  2. Heat water and jaggery; boil until a drop forms a solid lump in water.
  3. Switch off heat; stir in rice powder slowly to avoid lumps.
  4. Add Atta and cardamom powder to form the paga.
  5. Form circles, roll in sesame, and fry over medium heat.

Pudding

Velvety caramel custard steamed in a tiffin.

Pudding

Ingredients: 2c Milk, 4 eggs, 7tsp sugar, 1tsp vanilla. Caramel: 2tsp sugar, 1/2tsp oil.

  1. Melt caramel sugar and oil in tiffin box until brown; stop heat.
  2. Grind egg, sugar, vanilla, and milk; pour into tiffin.
  3. Steam in pressure cooker water bath (1/4 full).
  4. 1 whistle, then 10-20 mins slow flame. Flip when cool.

Watergate Salad

A refreshing pistachio and pineapple delight.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients: 4oz pistachio pudding mix, 20oz crushed pineapple, 1c marshmallow, 1/2c nuts, 1.75c whipped topping.

  1. Blend pudding mix, pineapple (with juice), marshmallows, and nuts.
  2. Gently fold in the whipped topping.
  3. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

Poda Pitha

Slow-baked traditional rice cake.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients: 4c Rice, 2c black gram, 1c coconut, 3c sugar/jaggery, ginger, cashew, ghee, cardamom.

  1. Soak rice/gram 4h; grind gram fine, rice coarse.
  2. Ferment 4h; mix with coconut, ginger, sugar, and cashews.
  3. Oil pressure cooker; pour batter.
  4. Bake on simmer (no whistle) for 1 hour.

Khira Gaintha

Milk dumplings flavored with coconut and nuts.

Khira Gaintha

Ingredients: 2c Rice flour, sugar, milk, cardamom, coconut, cashew, almonds.

  1. Boil water with salt/oil; stir in flour to make dough.
  2. Knead, form balls, and steam.
  3. Thicken milk with evaporated milk and sugar.
  4. Simmer balls in milk; add nuts and coconut. Serve cool.

Rashmalai

Spongy Rasagollas in a rich Kulfi-milk sauce.

Rashmalai

Ingredients: 1 box Rasagolla, 1 can evaporated milk, 1c milk, Gits Kulfi mix.

  1. Heat milk/evaporated milk; add Kulfi mix.
  2. Squeeze Rasagollas to remove syrup; drop into hot milk.
  3. Warm for 2 mins; chill before serving.

Rasagolla

Fresh homemade chenna balls in light syrup.

Rasagolla

Ingredients: 3L full cream milk, vinegar, 1.5tbsp flour, 1.5c sugar, cardamom.

  1. Curdle boiling milk with vinegar; strain and hang for 1h.
  2. Knead chenna with flour until smooth as dough.
  3. Boil balls in syrup: 12m high (lid on), 5m low (lid off).

Mango Pie

Creamy, no-bake mango cheese pie.

Mango Pie

Ingredients: 15oz Mango pulp, 1/2c sugar, 8oz cheese, 8oz Cool Whip, 2 jello pkts, crust.

  1. Mix cheese and sugar; add pulp and Cool Whip.
  2. Microwave jello in 1/2c water (20s); stir into mix.
  3. Pour into crust; chill for 24 hours.

Fruit Pudding

Instant mango cream with fresh fruit garnish.

Quick Fruit Pudding

Ingredients: 1 tin mango pulp, 1 tin condensed milk, 1 container whip cream, fruits.

  1. Whisk pulp and condensed milk; add whip cream.
  2. Mix well and garnish with fruits.
  3. Serve fresh or frozen.

Suji Cake

Semolina Pitta-style cake with buttermilk.

Suji Cake

Ingredients: 3c suji, 3c buttermilk, 3/4c oil, 3/4c sugar, 1tsp soda, 1c coconut, cardamom.

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Set aside on counter for 2 hours.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.

Cuban Cream Cake

Rishi’s rich whipped cream sponge cake.

Cuban Cream Cake

Ingredients: 2c Flour, baking powder, 1.5c sugar, 1.5c heavy cream, 3 eggs, vanilla.

  1. Preheat oven 350°F; grease two 9" pans.
  2. Whip cream until thick.
  3. Fold frothy eggs into cream; fold in sifted dry ingredients and vanilla.
  4. Bake for 30-40 mins until dry.

Rajma

Red kidney beans with a unique besan twist.

Rajma

Ingredients: 2c boiled Rajma, 1tbsp besan, ghee, chilli, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, jeera.

  1. Heat ghee; add bay leaf and jeera.
  2. Fry besan until reddish.
  3. Add ginger, tomato, and beans; cook to consistency.

Laau Ghanta

Bottle gourd stew with coconut and bengal beans.

Laau Ghanta

Ingredients: 12" Laau, potato, onion, coconut, bengal black beans, ghee, phutan, hingu.

  1. Heat ghee with phutan, bay leaf, and cardamom.
  2. Sauté onion/ginger; add gourd, potato, coconut, and beans.
  3. Cook until soft; add tomato, hingu, and coriander.
  4. Reduce on high flame to thicken gravy.

Besara

A variety of veggies in a sharp mustard paste.

Besara

Ingredients: Pumpkin, yam, banana, sweet potato, mustard/cumin paste, chickpeas, coconut.

  1. Grind mustard, cumin, ginger, and pepper into paste.
  2. Mix veggies with paste, coconut, chickpeas, and water; cook until done.
  3. Mix in jaggery.
  4. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, and red chillies.

Potala Kurma

Pointed gourd with soft chena and coconut milk.

Potala Kurma

Ingredients: Gourd, onion, ginger-garlic, yogurt, coconut milk, chena (from lemon/milk).

  1. Fry gourds with deep cuts; set aside.
  2. Sauté ginger-chili paste and spices; add yogurt/tomato.
  3. Add water, chena, and gourds; cook to consistency.
  4. Finish with cream garnish.

Laal Mutton

Fiery red meat curry slow-cooked in ghee.

Laal Mutton

Ingredients: 1kg Mutton, red chilli paste, ghee, curd, garlic, whole spices, coriander powder.

  1. Slow cook mutton in ghee with whole spices and onion.
  2. Add chilli paste and curd; simmer until meat is tender.
  3. Adjust salt to taste and serve with rice.

Poha Roll

Crunchy potato and poha snacks with roasted peanuts.

Poha Roll

Ingredients: 30g Poha, 100g potato, 200g peanut, onion, amchur, 1 egg.

  1. Mash potato; mix with soaked poha, onion, and peanuts.
  2. Form into rolls; dip in beaten egg.
  3. Fry until golden and crispy.

Kabab

Boiled then flash-fried tender meat kebabs.

Kabab

Ingredients: Ground chicken/turkey, ginger-garlic, curd, Chaplee masala, chaat masala, butter.

  1. Mix all; freeze for 30 mins.
  2. Make balls; boil in water for 10 mins.
  3. Strain, then flash-fry in butter. Sprinkle chaat masala.

Potol Rasha

Pointed gourds in a thick coconut milk gravy.

Potol Rasha

Ingredients: 500g Potol, coconut-ginger paste, coconut milk, panch phutan, ghee.

  1. Fry round-cut potol in ghee; set aside.
  2. Sauté masala paste with phutan and bay leaf until oil separates.
  3. Simmer potol with coconut milk and salt.

Malai Chicken

Double-marinated creamy chicken with mustard oil.

Malai Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, heavy cream, cheese, herb paste, tandoori masala, mustard oil.

  1. 1st Marination: Lemon, salt, ginger-garlic (30m).
  2. 2nd Marination: Hung curd, cream, cheese, spices (overnight).
  3. Broil in oven, flipping until golden.

Coconut Fried Rice

Aromatic rice soaked and cooked in coconut milk.

Coconut Fried Rice

Ingredients: 1c Rice, 1.5c coconut milk, 0.5c water, shahi jeera, salt.

  1. Soak all four ingredients together for 45 mins.
  2. Cook the mixture with salt until rice is tender and milk is absorbed.

Birria

Mexican pulled goat served with homemade tortillas.

Birria

Ingredients: 4lb goat, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, ginger, broth, roasted tomatoes, garlic.

  1. Brown meat; sauté onion/garlic/spices.
  2. Blend sauce with charred tomatoes and broth.
  3. Pressure cook on HIGH (45m).
  4. Shred meat into the consommé. Serve with flour/atta tortillas.
