Arisha Pitha

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Rice
  • 2 cups Jaggery
  • ¼ cup Water
  • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • 1 cup Atta (wheat flour)
  • Cardamom powder

Instructions:

Soak rice overnight, grind into powder. Boil jaggery in water until it forms a solid lump in cold water. Mix in rice powder and atta slowly to avoid lumps. Add cardamom. Shape into small circles, roll in sesame, and fry.

Poda Pitha

Ingredients:

  • Rice & Skinless Black Gram (Biri)
  • Jaggery & Cardamom
  • Small pieces of Coconut & Ginger
  • Salt

Instructions:

Soak and grind rice and biri separately, then mix and ferment. Add ginger, jaggery, coconut, and cardamom. Traditionally slow-cooked in a tiffin box or pressure cooker until the outer layer is charred ("poda").

Birria (Goat)

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 lbs Goat meat
  • Cinnamon sticks & Cloves
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Fire-roasted tomatoes & Chicken broth
  • Vinegar & Oregano

Instructions:

Sauté aromatics, blend with tomatoes and broth to make a sauce. Pressure cook goat meat in the sauce for 45 minutes until fall-apart tender. Shred the meat and serve in the consommé.

Besara

Ingredients:

  • Pumpkin, Yam, Raw Banana, Sweet Potato
  • Mustard & Cumin seeds
  • Garlic, Red chili & Turmeric
  • Ambula (Dried Mango) or Tamarind

Instructions:

Grind mustard, cumin, garlic, and chili into a smooth paste. Boil vegetables with turmeric and salt. Stir in the mustard paste and add ambula for tanginess. Simmer until veggies are tender.

Watergate Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can Crushed Pineapple
  • 1 box Pistachio Pudding mix
  • 1 cup Miniature Marshmallows
  • 1 cup Chopped Pecans
  • 1 container Whipped Topping

Instructions:

Combine pineapple (with juice), pudding mix, and marshmallows in a bowl. Fold in the whipped topping and pecans. Chill for at least one hour before serving.
